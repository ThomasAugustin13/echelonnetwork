Detailed Offerings

Our Service Verticals

Phoenix Elevator Parts maintains strict quality controls across every service vertical.

Elevator Installation

Comprehensive installations covering Passenger Lifts, Hospital Lifts, Home Lifts, Corporate Lifts, and heavy-duty Goods & Service Lifts. We handle shaft preparation audits, machinery alignment, control panel wiring, and safety load tests.

Elevator Maintenance (AMC)

Keep your lifts operational with Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC). Our services include:
• Routine safety checks
• Smooth operation testing
• Lubrication & alignment adjustments
• 24/7 emergency breakdown dispatch
• Wear-and-tear parts replacement

Elevator Modernization

Upgrades for outdated elevators to improve comfort, speed, energy savings, and safety features. We install:
• High-efficiency control panels & gearless motors
• Sleek, modern interior cabins
• Dynamic door sensors & governors
• Low-power standby lighting

Repair & Support Services

If your elevator displays error codes or runs roughly, our quick-response team resolves mechanical and electrical issues. We troubleshoot door mechanisms, rope tensions, control relays, and drive inverter faults.

Customized Elevator Design

Tailor-made designs optimized for your spatial constraints and decor preferences:
• Custom cabin dimensions & load configurations
• Distinctive cabin wall finishes, steel linings, and handrails
• Customizable control panels & displays

Phoenix Elevator Parts provides safe, efficient, and modern elevator solutions for homes, industries, and commercial buildings with trusted performance and certified components.
