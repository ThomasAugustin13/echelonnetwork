Who We Are

Engineering Elevation. Delivering Reliability.

Phoenix Elevator Parts delivers advanced elevator and lifting solutions for residential, commercial, industrial, and institutional spaces. With strong engineering expertise and a focus on innovation, we create elevators that ensure maximum safety, smooth comfort, and reliable vertical mobility.

We offer manufacturing, installation, modernization, and maintenance services for Passenger Lifts, Home Elevators, Goods Lifts, Hospital Lifts, Capsule Lifts, and Custom Lifting Systems. With quality craftsmanship, advanced technology, and dependable service, we deliver long-lasting performance and enhance mobility across every level.

Our Journey

Milestones of Vertical Excellence

A history of engineering, safety innovation, and high-performance elevator development.

2012

Inception & Door Systems

Founded Bangalore assembly lines specializing in high-strength, precise elevator landing door mechanisms and automatic sliders.

2016

Traction Expansion

Expanded local design capabilities to include gearless Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor (PMSM) systems and hydraulic lifts.

2021

Certified Safety Gears

Achieved full compliance certificates for EN 81 safety codes and launched dual electromagnetic progressive safety brakes.

2025

IoT & Cloud Telemetry

Integrated micro-controllers with wireless cloud servers, releasing smartphone calling interfaces and fingerprint credentials.
