Capsule Lifts

Home / Products / Capsule Lifts
Architectural Landmark

Elegantly Designed Panoramic Glass Capsule Elevators

Our Capsule Lifts are engineered to serve as architectural centerpieces. Featuring premium curved or polygonal laminated glass walls, these elevators provide riders with panoramic external views while upgrading the visual identity of shopping plazas, luxury showrooms, corporate offices, and modern duplex homes.

Powered by highly efficient, whisper-quiet Permanent Magnet Gearless PMSM machines, our capsule cabins run smoothly, eliminating starts and stops vibration. Each lift can be customized with under-car spot lights, decorative ceiling grids, and mirror-finished gold or rose stainless steel panel outlines.

Architectural Specifications

Shapes: Semi-Circular, Square, Hexagonal
Glass: Laminated Multi-layer Safety Glass
Speed: 0.5 m/s to 1.75 m/s
Accents: Under-car LEDs / Rose Gold Frame
Luxury & Style

Premium Panoramic Advantages

Combine vertical transit efficiency with spectacular spatial visual accents.

270-Degree Panoramic View

High-transparency heavy tempered safety glass panels offer broad panoramic views, making vertical travel an enjoyable, scenic experience.

Custom Aesthetic Outlines

Available in custom finishes including mirror gold, hairline copper, stainless steel, and colorful canopy accents to match any structural branding.

Whisper-Quiet Operation

By using advanced PMSM drives and low-friction guide shoes, cabin runs are completely silent, smooth, and free of vertical vibrations.

Redundant Safety Glass

Constructed with multi-layered, shatterproof PVB laminated glass panels. If any minor damage occurs, the glass stays intact without shedding fragments.

Illuminated Canopy & Base

Custom-configured under-car spotlight modules and top glowing crowns create an eye-catching illuminated structure moving up and down.

Flexible Shaft Adaptability

Can be installed inside traditional masonry shafts or open steel-and-glass structural frames to enhance outdoor views or building lobbies.
