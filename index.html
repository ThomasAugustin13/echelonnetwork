FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our installations, certifications, and service terms.

What types of elevator services does Phoenix Elevator Parts provide?

We provide comprehensive vertical transportation solutions, including manufacturing, engineering design, installation, modernization, and maintenance (AMC) for Passenger Elevators, Home Lifts, Hospital Elevators, Goods Lifts, and Hydraulic Lifts.

How long does it take to install a new elevator?

Standard installations generally take 4 to 8 weeks depending on building height, shaft clearance, customization levels, and site readiness. This timeframe includes thorough quality and safety load tests.

Do you offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)?

Yes, we offer flexible AMC options (Standard and Comprehensive) which cover regular monthly checkups, lubrication adjustment, safety tests, breakdown response, and parts repair or replacement.

Can we modernize an existing older elevator?

Absolutely. We specialize in elevator modernization, replacing older mechanical parts with high-efficiency VVVF drives, upgrading electronic controllers, and modernizing cabin interior linings for a fresher appearance and smoother ride.
