Safe, Smooth, and Comfortable Vertical Transport for Every Building

At Phoenix Elevator Parts, our Passenger Elevators are designed to provide reliable, comfortable, and efficient vertical transportation for residential, commercial, and office buildings. Engineered for smooth operation and user safety, these elevators enhance convenience and accessibility for all occupants.

With durable components, advanced control systems, and stylish cabin designs, our Passenger Elevators offer quiet, stable, and energy-efficient performance. Available in multiple capacities and finishes, they are tailored to match your building’s architecture while ensuring maximum safety and reliability.

Technical Specifications

Load Capacities: 225kg to 1275kg
Speed: Up to 2.0 m/s
Door Open Type: Side/Center Auto
Shaft Type: Traction / MRL
Benefits

Why Choose Our Passenger Elevator?

Passenger elevators are designed to transport people smoothly, safely, and comfortably.

Modern & Stylish Cabins

Elegant cabin finishes in hairline stainless steel, architectural glass, and custom lighting to complement your building's interior styling.

Smooth & Quiet Operation

Advanced VVVF variable frequency drives and isolation dampening assure vibration-free, silent travel.

High Load Capacity

Manufactured with flexible cabin sizes and weight tolerances, catering to both low-traffic homes and high-traffic towers.

Enhanced Safety Features

Includes emergency battery lowering systems, electronic overload warnings, mechanical safety locks, and fire emergency returns.

Energy Efficient

Equipped with green gearless traction machines and automatic standby controls that shut down cabin fans and lights during idle hours.

Low Maintenance

Heavy-duty, wear-resistant parts and modular wiring ensure rapid diagnostic runs and minimal annual service downtime.
