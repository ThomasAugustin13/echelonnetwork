Next-Gen Technology

Intelligent Vertical Mobility Connected to Your Lifestyle

Our IoT Smart Lifts represent the absolute pinnacle of elevator technology. By integrating the lift controller with local networks and cloud servers, we provide a touchless, highly secure, and optimized transportation solution for corporate offices, high-end apartments, and smart homes.

Passengers can authorize entry via face scanning or fingerprint biometrics and dispatch their lift directly from their mobile phones. Backed by real-time sensor monitoring, the elevator predicts maintenance needs, minimizing downtime and optimizing traffic flow.

Why Upgrade to a Connected Elevator System?

Maximize security, efficiency, and comfort with our smart elevator software suite.

Touchless Operations

Call and control the elevator from your smartphone. Perfect for keeping transit hygienic, convenient, and completely hands-free.

Secure Biometric Access

Restricts elevator access to authorized personnel or residents using high-speed facial recognition cameras and fingerprint panels.

Predictive Cloud Maintenance

Real-time diagnostics continually record parameters, sending automated system error logs to our engineers before a breakdown happens.
