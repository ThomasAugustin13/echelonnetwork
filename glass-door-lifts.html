Modern Aesthetics

Luxury Automatic Sliding Glass Door Lifts

Our Glass Door Lifts are designed to blend structural function with beautiful visual transparency. By using automatic glass panels instead of solid metal sheets, these lifts brighten passenger cabins with natural ambient light and allow the cabin to blend seamlessly with premium building lobbies and residential duplex styles.

Each glass door system is driven by a specialized VVVF door operator that controls speed curves dynamically for silent and smooth opening/closing transitions. We use multi-layer laminated safety glass mounted inside high-strength structural aluminum or stainless steel frames, providing excellent durability and absolute passenger protection.

Door Technical Features

Center Open & Side Open Sliding
Framed or Frameless Glass Panels
Full Height Infrared Safety Beam
Smart VVVF Synchronous Operator
Design Advantages

Why Choose Glass Doors For Your Lift?

Elevate the ambient design of your property with premium automatic glass interfaces.

Enhanced Luminous Space

Allows natural and lobby light to enter the cabin, creating a bright, spacious, and comfortable riding atmosphere.

Premium Interior Harmony

Available in transparent glass, tinted bronze/smoke, and patterns to match lobby wall coverings and wooden finishes.

Extremely Silent Operation

Precision hangers and synthetic rubber belt tracks absorb operational vibrations, creating near-silent door opening cycles.

Laminated Security Tempered

Constructed with multi-layered safety glass. It offers outstanding mechanical resistance and stays completely integrated even under impact.

Intelligent Obstacle Detection

Equipped with a high-density infrared light curtain. Doors reverse open immediately upon sensing any obstacle near the entry.

Enhanced Property Valuation

A premium glass door configuration is a modern design feature that increases the valuation and luxury quotient of your villa or complex.
