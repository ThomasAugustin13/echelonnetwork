Breathe New Life into Your Aging Lift System

At Phoenix Elevator Parts, we specialize in complete and custom-tailored Elevator Modernization. If your existing lift system is experiencing frequent breakdowns, rough rides, high energy bills, or is still using dangerous manual gates, our technical engineers can overhaul it to modern standards.

Instead of going through the massive cost and structural disruption of installing an entirely new elevator system, we target the mechanical, electrical, and aesthetic cores. We replace obsolete controllers, install automatic sliding doors, and mount eco-friendly gearless motors, ensuring maximum safety, comfort, and compliance.

Manual to Auto Door Upgrade

We replace unsafe collapsible manual gates or swing doors with modern, automatic sliding doors (telescopic or center-opening). This involves installing door headers, variable-frequency door operators, and modern cabin door panels, completely eliminating door-opening hazards.

Smart Controller & Drive Overhaul

We replace obsolete electromagnetic relays with advanced microprocessor control panels integrated with VVVF drives. This ensures smooth acceleration, resolves leveling issues that cause tripping, and manages energy efficiency by entering stand-by mode when idle.

Challenges in Old Lifts & Our Solutions

Understanding the risks of legacy elevator shafts and how modern engineering fixes them.

Risks of Outdated Elevators

Unsafe Manual Collapsible Gates

Passengers, especially children, can accidentally open gates while the lift is in motion, leading to severe crushing hazards.

Uneven Landing & Leveling (Tripping Hazard)

Worn brakes and control relays fail to stop the cabin exactly level with the floor, causing passengers to trip and fall.

Frequent Breakdowns & High Maintenance Costs

Locating rare replacement parts for obsolete controllers leads to prolonged elevator downtime and high emergency repair bills.

High Power Consumption & Noise

Old geared induction motors draw massive starting currents, spiking building energy bills, vibrating rooms, and causing loud hums.

The Phoenix Modernization Cure

Automatic Safe Doors & Light Curtains
