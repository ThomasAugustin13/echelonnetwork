Hospital Elevators

Home / Products / Hospital Elevators
Clinical & Reliable

Smooth, Vibration-Free Transport for Bed Patients & Stretchers

In medical facilities, elevator operations can directly impact critical care. At Phoenix Elevator Parts, our Hospital Elevators are engineered to meet the highest safety, hygiene, and transit criteria. They feature smooth starting and stopping curves to prevent discomfort for bedridden patients.

Designed with wide door openings and deep cabin spaces, they easily accommodate standard stretcher sizes, hospital beds, and wheel chairs alongside medical personnel. The controllers feature dedicated medical emergency priority override systems.

Technical Specifications

Capacity: 15 to 26 Persons (1025kg-1600kg)
Leveling Accuracy: ± 2mm (Ultra-precise)
Door Config: Telescopic / Center auto opening
Material: Anti-bacterial Hairline SS 304
Clinical Benefits

Engineered for Healthcare Environments

Precision-focused features that prioritize health and safety.

Smooth Acceleration Curves

Utilizes fine-tuned frequency drives that prevent abrupt stops, vibration, or jerks, preserving patient comfort.

Emergency Priority Mode

Equipped with a key-operated medical override system, allowing doctors to bypass general hall calls for emergency runs.

Hygienic Steel Surfaces

Finished with premium SS 304 scratch-resistant hairline steel, supporting easy cleaning and chemical disinfections.

Precise Level Adjustment

Maintains leveling alignment to prevent trip hazards when rolling stretchers or wheel chairs across the sill.

Full ARD Redundancy

Reliable backup batteries bring the cabin to the closest exit floor during electrical outages, keeping ventilation fans active.

Delayed Door Controls

Features adjustable door open timing and proximity sensors, ensuring safe entry for patients with limited mobility.
